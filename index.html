001 — La Filosofia

A cup of coffee,
poured with honor.

When Marco & Sofia Castelli opened Fortezza, they wanted Fort Wayne — Ke-Ki On-Ga, the old confluence — to feel what they grew up with in Northern Italy. A café where the barista knows your name, where coffee is poured slowly and savored. Not fuel. A small ceremony.

002 — What We Pour

Three things.
Done the old way.

01 — Caffè  ·  02 — Dolci  ·  03 — Benvenuti

/01

Caffè Vergnano.

We pour Caffè Vergnano — Turin's oldest roaster, founded in 1882. Pulled on a traditional lever machine, the way Marco's nonno taught him in the Piedmont.

  • Espresso · Macchiato · Cortado
  • Cappuccino · Flat White · Latte
  • Affogato · Iced Shakerato
  • Drip · Pour-over · Cold Brew
/02

Dolci Italiani.

Sfogliatelle from a Naples-trained baker. Cornetti baked at 5am. Tiramisù in a coppa. Cannoli filled to order — no soggy shells, no shortcuts.

  • Cornetto — Italian croissant
  • Sfogliatella — Naples pastry
  • Cannolo Siciliano — filled fresh
  • Tiramisù & biscotti
/03

Benvenuti.

A small fortress of warm light and worn wood. We greet every guest. Bring your laptop. Bring your kids. Bring your father-in-law. We've got you a table.

  • Free Wi-Fi · plenty of outlets
  • Sidewalk patio in season
  • Dog-friendly outside
  • Curbside & pickup orders

003 — The Beans

From Turin.
To East Berry.

The Vergnano family has been roasting in Turin since 1882 — perfected in the espresso bars of the Piedmont, where coffee is poured fast and pulled correctly. We're proud to bring it to Ke-Ki On-Ga.

004 — Inside the Fortezza

What you'll find.
All day, every day.

01

Espresso Bar

Single, double, ristretto. Pulled on demand and served in a warm porcelain cup.

02

Pastry Case

Cornetti, sfogliatelle, cannoli, biscotti, tiramisù. Made fresh — never frozen, never reheated.

03

Panini Press

Prosciutto & mozzarella, caprese, breakfast egg-and-pancetta on warm focaccia.

04

Gelato Bar

Twelve flavors made in-house weekly — pistachio, stracciatella, fior di latte, espresso.

05

Drip + Cold Brew

Two daily roasts and a 24-hour cold brew. Iced espresso tonics in summer.

06

Tea Selection

Loose-leaf English breakfast, earl grey, chamomile, matcha, chai.

07

Free Wi-Fi

Strong signal, plenty of outlets. Stay an hour. Stay all afternoon.

08

Beans to Go

Take Vergnano home. Whole bean or ground for your machine, brewer, or moka pot.
